THE CONCEPT

(I found this cute little guy here.)

This is an art project disguised as capitalism disguised as cat content.

Or maybe it's cat content disguised as capitalism disguised as art.

Either way: I photograph street cats in Cyprus. You subscribe on Patreon. Your money buys food for those cats and an opportunity for me to do photography full time.

"It's like a subscription box, except instead of getting socks you get the satisfaction of knowing a cat ate today because of you. And you get cool photos."

HOW IT WORKS

1

I WALK & SHOOT

I walk around Cyprus with my camera. I find cats on the streets. I feed and photograph them. Sometimes they're photogenic. Sometimes they're not. I feed them anyway.

2

YOU PAY

You join my Patreon. Choose your tier. Your subscription buys cat food. Not metaphorical cat food. Actual cat food. From an actual store.

3

EVERYONE WINS

You get exclusive photos & videos. The cats get fed. I get to keep wandering around with a camera. It's a tiny economy that somehow makes sense.
